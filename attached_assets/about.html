About Millikit

Discover our story and mission to promote healthy living through traditional grains

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Millikit emerged from a passion for traditional Indian grains and a commitment to promoting healthy living. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make nutritious, high-quality millets accessible to everyone while supporting local farmers and sustainable agriculture practices.

We work directly with farmers across India who practice organic farming methods, ensuring that every grain meets our strict quality standards while providing fair compensation to the farming community.

Our Mission

Promote Health

Making nutritious millets accessible to everyone, encouraging healthier dietary choices.

Support Farmers

Partnering with local farmers to promote sustainable agriculture and fair trade practices.

Preserve Tradition

Keeping traditional farming methods alive while embracing modern sustainable practices.
